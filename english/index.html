Welcome to Dernière Ronde!

A board game night always ends with a last round, but it starts well before that.

Our objective is to make players want to discover your new game, to make yourself known as a designer, and to promote publishers who love the world of board games.

By presenting your game’s illustrations, the beauty of the components, and mechanics we encounter during a game, our photos and videos will help your projects reach the next level.

We are looking forward to discovering your games and doing business with you.

Marc-André Larivière
